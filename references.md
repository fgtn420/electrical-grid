## Dataset

- **UCI Electrical Grid Stability Simulated Dataset**
  Arzamasov, V., Böhm, K., & Jochem, P. (2018). *Towards Concise Models of Grid Stability*. In Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Communications, Control, and Computing Technologies for Smart Grids (SmartGridComm).
  URL: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/471/electrical+grid+stability+simulated+data
  Licence: CC BY 4.0
  *Used as the primary dataset for binary stability classification and adversarial analysis.*

## Papers

- **Carlini & Wagner (2017) — CW attack**

  *Basis for the CW-L1 attack formulation: min-perturbation objective with confidence margin kappa.*
